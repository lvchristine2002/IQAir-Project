This website was developed to provide real-time air quality, and temperature, information globally using data from the IQAir API.


Many people suffer from some kind of breathing complications, such as Asthma, and the air quality affects their breathing more than others. This website hopes to provide reliable air quality information in order to give these people the information they need in order to either prevent any complications due to air quality or to allow people to plan their day accordingly.


It also includes temperature information that is useful for anybody looking for information about how the weather will be for that day, similar to the weather app for iPhone.
